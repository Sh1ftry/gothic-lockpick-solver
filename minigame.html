HOW TO USE

Select a plate — click or tap any plate to select it (highlighted in blue).

Slide a plate — drag the selected plate left or right. The coloured circle stays fixed; the plate moves under it. The circle turns red when centred, gold when off-centre.

Program other plates — when a plate is selected, every other plate shows two tap zones on its edges:

  O (left edge) — marks that plate to move in the opposite direction to the selected plate.
  S (right edge) — marks that plate to move in the same direction as the selected plate.

Tap O or S to toggle the setting. The programming is stored per selected plate — switching selection and coming back will keep your settings.
